I'm Nicole, a student, artist, and writer from San Jose. The hero page is a play on words; my name, 田梓彤/ Tiánzǐ Tóng, bears phonetic resemblance to 田子坊/ Tiánzǐ Fāng, a Shanghai residential area that has now been transformed into modern art studios and an upscale shopping district. Welcome to this assorted subsets of things I find beautiful!
